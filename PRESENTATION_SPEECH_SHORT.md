## THE PROBLEM (30 seconds)

Students today face four critical challenges:

1. **Information Overload** - Multiple courses, assignments, projects, extracurriculars. Traditional to-do lists can't handle it.

2. **Burnout** - Over 60% of college students report feeling overwhelmed by their workload.

3. **One-Size-Fits-All Apps** - Most productivity tools treat a 15-minute homework assignment the same as a 3-hour deep work session. But they're NOT the same.

4. **No Learning Loop** - We complete tasks but never understand our patterns. When are we most productive? How does sleep affect performance? Nobody tells us.

---

## THE SOLUTION (40 seconds)

**PlanWise** is your personal AI productivity coach that understands student life.

**Smart Task Management**: Not all tasks are equal . Tag tasks by priority (low to urgent), category (study, coding, fitness), cognitive load (1-10), and whether it requires deep work. Our AI schedules tasks during YOUR peak productivity hours.

**Sleep Tracking**: Log sleep hours, rate quality, track sleep debt. We correlate your sleep patterns with productivity - notice you crush it after 8 hours? The app reminds you.

**Skill Development**: Every task builds a skill. Coding assignments increase your programming hours. Fitness goals grow your health stats. Watch yourself level up from beginner to expert.

**Gamification That Matters**: Earn points, build streaks, unlock achievements tied to REAL accomplishments. "Sleep Champion" means 30 days of healthy sleep habits, not arbitrary badges.

---

## THE DASHBOARD (30 seconds)

Everything comes together in your personalized dashboard:
- Current streak and total points
- Sleep debt over the last week  
- Task completion rate
- Today's pending tasks
- AI-generated insights about YOUR productivity patterns

The dashboard learns from you. It might notice you're most productive Tuesday mornings, or that good sleep equals more completed tasks. It's like having a data scientist analyzing your productivity 24/7.
