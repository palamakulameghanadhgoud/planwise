## ğŸ’¡ Tips to Maximize Points

1. **Set Accurate Priorities**: Don't mark everything as urgent, but don't undersell difficult tasks either.

2. **Mark Deep Work Correctly**: If a task requires uninterrupted focus (studying, coding, writing), mark it as deep work.

3. **Assess Cognitive Load Honestly**: 
   - 1-3: Simple, routine tasks
   - 4-6: Moderate difficulty
   - 7-9: Challenging, requires mental effort
   - 10: Extremely difficult

4. **Break Large Tasks**: Instead of one 5-hour task, break it into smaller chunks. This gives you:
   - More frequent wins
   - Better point optimization
   - Clearer progress tracking

5. **Bundle Priorities**: Complete urgent tasks when you're at peak energy to maximize both productivity and points!
